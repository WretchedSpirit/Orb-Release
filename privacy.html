Orb Release Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wretched Games handles information in connection with Orb Release. If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the game.

1. Publisher Information

Orb Release is published by Wretched Games. Website: WretchedStudio.com. Privacy and legal contact: LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com. Bug reports: BugReport@wretchedstudio.com.

2. Information Stored by the Game

Orb Release stores gameplay and settings data to operate the game. This may include currency balances, gems, keys, cards, abilities, upgrades, equipped loadouts, stage progress, tutorial progress, settings, legal acceptance, rewarded ad limits, purchase entitlement flags, player display name, local player ID, and local save snapshots.

Most gameplay data is stored locally on your device. If platform sign-in or cloud save features are used, save-related information may also be processed through the platform provider, such as Google Play Games.

3. Information You Provide

You may choose to provide information when contacting Wretched Games, such as your email address, bug reports, screenshots, device details, diagnostics, or descriptions of gameplay issues. This information is used to respond to you, investigate bugs, improve the game, and maintain records of support or legal requests.

4. Platform and Service Providers

Orb Release may use third-party platform and service providers, including Google Play, Google Play Games Services, Google Play Billing, Unity, Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, analytics, crash reporting, advertising, in-app purchase, hosting, and development services. These providers may collect or process information according to their own policies and SDK behavior.

Depending on the services enabled, provider processing may include device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, ad interactions, approximate location, diagnostics, crash data, performance data, purchase information, entitlement information, Google Play Games profile or player identifiers, saved game data, and anti-fraud or security signals.

5. Ads

Orb Release may show optional rewarded ads. Ad providers and mediation services may use device and advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, diagnostics, performance data, and other signals to deliver, measure, limit, secure, and improve ads. Rewarded ads are optional, and rewards are granted only after completion is confirmed by the ad provider.

Some testing, closed testing, prerelease, or regional builds may have live ads disabled. If ads are disabled, ad-related functionality may be unavailable.

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Orb Release may offer in-app purchases through Google Play. Payment and purchase processing is handled by Google Play. Wretched Games may receive purchase confirmation, entitlement, subscription, and transaction-related information needed to grant consumable items, non-consumable items, subscriptions, restore purchases, prevent fraud, and provide support.

7. Google Play Games and Cloud Save

If you use Google Play Games sign-in or cloud save features, Google and Wretched Games may process information needed to identify your game profile, connect your local save to your platform account, save progress, restore progress, and troubleshoot sync issues. Local saves still work when platform sign-in or cloud save features are unavailable.

8. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate the game, save progress, restore purchases, grant purchases and rewards, maintain player settings, support cloud save features, improve performance, diagnose crashes or bugs, prevent fraud or abuse, comply with legal and platform requirements, and show, limit, secure, or measure optional rewarded ads.

9. Sharing

Wretched Games may share or make information available to service providers that help operate Orb Release, including platform, payment, advertising, mediation, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, and development services. Wretched Games may also disclose information if required by law, platform policy, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, security, or game integrity.

10. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless deleted by you, the operating system, or the game. If cloud save or platform services are used, related data may remain with the relevant provider according to that provider's policies. Support emails and bug reports may be retained as long as reasonably needed for support, debugging, legal, security, and recordkeeping purposes.

11. Your Choices

You can delete local game data by using in-game reset features where available, clearing app data, or uninstalling the game. Device and Google settings may allow you to limit ad tracking, reset advertising identifiers, manage app permissions, control diagnostics sharing, manage Google Play Games profile settings, manage subscriptions, or request refunds through Google Play.

12. Children

Orb Release is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required by applicable law. Wretched Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com.

13. Security

Wretched Games uses reasonable measures appropriate for a small independent game to protect information. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, and no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to prevent all unauthorized access.

14. Third-Party Policies

Third-party services process information under their own policies. You can review policies and terms from providers such as Google Play, Google Play Games, Google Payments, Unity, Unity Ads, and Unity LevelPlay on their official websites.

15. Changes

Wretched Games may update this Privacy Policy as Orb Release changes or as legal, platform, advertising, analytics, purchase, or cloud save requirements change. The updated date at the top shows when this policy was last revised.

16. Contact

For privacy or legal questions, contact LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com. For bug reports, contact BugReport@wretchedstudio.com.
