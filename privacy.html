Orb Release Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wretched Games handles information in connection with Orb Release. If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the game.

1. Publisher Information

Orb Release is published by Wretched Games. Website: WretchedStudio.com. Privacy and legal contact: LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com.

2. Information We Collect Directly

Orb Release is designed to avoid collecting directly identifying personal information from you inside the game. The game may store local gameplay data on your device, such as currency balances, cards, upgrades, settings, legal acceptance, rewarded ad limits, and purchase-related entitlement flags.

3. Information Collected by Platforms and Service Providers

Orb Release may use third-party platform and service providers, including Google Play, Unity, Unity Ads, Unity LevelPlay, analytics, crash reporting, advertising, and in-app purchase services. These providers may collect or process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, approximate location, diagnostics, crash data, purchase information, ad interactions, and performance data according to their own policies and SDK behavior.

4. Ads

Orb Release may show optional rewarded ads. Ad providers may use device and advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, and other signals to deliver, measure, limit, and improve ads. Rewarded ads are optional and rewards are granted only after completion is confirmed by the ad provider.

5. In-App Purchases

Orb Release may offer in-app purchases through Google Play. Payment and purchase processing is handled by Google Play. Wretched Games may receive purchase confirmation or entitlement information needed to grant consumable items, such as gems, or non-consumable items, such as remove ads.

6. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate the game, save progress, grant purchases and rewards, maintain player settings, improve performance, diagnose crashes or bugs, prevent fraud or abuse, comply with platform requirements, and show or measure optional rewarded ads.

7. Sharing

Wretched Games may share or make information available to service providers that help operate Orb Release, including platform, payment, advertising, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, and development services. We may also disclose information if required by law, platform policy, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

8. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless deleted by you, the operating system, or the game. Third-party providers retain information according to their own policies and legal requirements.

9. Children

Orb Release is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com.

10. Your Choices

You can delete local game data by clearing app data or uninstalling the game. Device settings may allow you to limit ad tracking, reset advertising identifiers, manage app permissions, or control diagnostics sharing. Google Play purchase history and refunds are managed through Google Play.

11. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate for a small independent game to protect information, but no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Orb Release changes or as legal, platform, advertising, analytics, or purchase requirements change. The updated date at the top shows when the policy was last revised.

13. Contact

For privacy or legal questions, contact LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com. For bug reports, contact BugReport@wretchedstudio.com.
