Orb Release Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-13

This Privacy Policy explains how Wretched Studio handles information for Orb Release. Orb Release is an Android mobile game distributed through Google Play.

1. Publisher Information

Orb Release is published by Wretched Studio. Website: WretchedStudio.com. Privacy and legal contact: LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com. Bug reports: BugReport@wretchedstudio.com.

2. Game Save Data

Orb Release stores game save data locally on your device. The save includes player ID, local player ID, platform provider, Google Play Games player ID, player display name, coins, gems, keys, upgrade levels, active upgrade levels, unlocked stage, card slots, camera settings, vibration setting, music setting, sound effects setting, text size, autoplay settings, tutorial progress, owned abilities, owned cards, unlocked cards, equipped cards, and enabled abilities.

Orb Release stores local ad reward counters, purchase entitlement flags, legal acceptance, and cloud save status in local device storage. The free gem rewarded ad counter tracks the date and the number of free gem ads watched that day.

3. Google Play Games Sign-In

Orb Release uses Google Play Games sign-in. When signed in, Orb Release uses the Google Play Games player ID and display name for account identity and account saves. When not signed in, Orb Release creates a local player ID and stores the local display name on the device.

4. Account Saves

Orb Release saves account progress through Google Play Games saved games for signed-in players. The account save contains the same structured game save data listed above. Local saves continue to work without sign-in.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

Orb Release sells Gem Packs, Remove Ads, and a VIP Subscription through Google Play. Google Play processes payment, subscription, refund, and purchase history information. Orb Release receives purchase confirmation, entitlement, subscription, and product information needed to grant gems, Remove Ads, VIP, restore purchases, prevent fraud, and provide support.

6. Ads

Orb Release shows skippable ads every 3 levels and limits regular ads to 1 ad every 5 minutes. Orb Release also offers optional rewarded ads. Rewards are granted only after the ad provider confirms the ad was completed.

Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, and mediated ad providers process advertising identifiers, device information, app activity, ad interactions, approximate location, diagnostics, performance data, and security signals to load, show, limit, measure, and secure ads.

7. Unity Analytics

Orb Release uses Unity Cloud Analytics. Unity Analytics processes app activity, device information, performance information, session information, diagnostics, and gameplay events to help Wretched Studio understand crashes, performance, balance, and player behavior.

8. Support and Bug Reports

When you contact Wretched Studio, you provide the information included in your message. This can include email address, screenshots, device details, diagnostics, gameplay descriptions, and bug details. Wretched Studio uses support messages to respond, investigate bugs, improve Orb Release, and keep support records.

9. Sharing

Wretched Studio shares information with service providers that operate Orb Release. These providers include Google Play, Google Play Games Services, Google Play Billing, Unity, Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, mediated ad networks, Unity Analytics, crash/diagnostic services, hosting services, and development services.

Wretched Studio also discloses information when required by law, legal process, platform policy, security needs, fraud prevention, or protection of Wretched Studio rights and game integrity.

10. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you reset progress, clear app data, or uninstall Orb Release. Google Play Games save data remains with Google Play Games according to Google policies and your Google account settings. Support emails and bug reports are kept for support, debugging, legal, security, and recordkeeping needs.

11. Your Choices

You can reset local progress in Orb Release, clear app data, uninstall the game, manage Google Play Games settings, manage Google account settings, reset the Android advertising ID, limit ad personalization in Android settings, manage subscriptions through Google Play, request refunds through Google Play, and contact Wretched Studio support.

12. All Ages

Orb Release is designed for all ages. Players under the legal age for online services in their region need permission from a parent or guardian to use Google Play Games, make purchases, watch ads, or contact Wretched Studio.

13. Security

Wretched Studio uses reasonable security measures for an independent mobile game. No storage, account, analytics, advertising, or support system is completely secure.

14. Third-Party Policies

Google Play, Google Play Games, Google Payments, Unity, Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, and mediated ad providers process information under their own policies. Review their official policies for details about their data practices.

15. Changes

Wretched Studio updates this Privacy Policy when Orb Release changes or when platform, advertising, analytics, purchase, legal, or cloud save requirements change. The date at the top shows the latest revision.

16. Contact

For privacy or legal questions, contact LegalServices@wretchedstudio.com. For bug reports, contact BugReport@wretchedstudio.com.
